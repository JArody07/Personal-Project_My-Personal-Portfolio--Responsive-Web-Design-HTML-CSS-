About Me

I’m Jaser, a passionate Computer Science student and aspiring Data Engineer. My journey began in 2022, two years after high school. My path into technology and data wasn’t the most conventional, but I've been persistent to continue pushing to achieve my dreams. I embrace the unpredictability of my career path, viewing each twist and turn as a valuable part of my growth rather than a setback. I think most of us have felt lost or have questioned their life choices, I know I have but I encourage others to see these as signs of exploration and development, not failure.

I’m drawn to Data because I'm a firm believer that it’s much more than just numbers, it’s the passion in storytelling through data that reveals powerful insights about our lives and society. This field combines curiosity and creativity, sparking new discoveries with every dataset along with powerful data systems that make all the magic happen. My goal is to make Data Analytics & Engineering accessible and inspiring, bridging the gap between knowledge and perseverance so others can overcome doubt and realize their own dreams just like I;m doing.

I invite anyone, whether a seasoned expert or beginner, to join me in exploring data’s mysteries, celebrating progress, and pushing boundaries together. I think that learning together is a foundation for success, and life by itself is a journey of endless learning. If you resonate with my journey or insights, I’d love to connect and hear your thoughts!
